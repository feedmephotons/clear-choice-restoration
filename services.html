Storm Damage Specialists

Indianapolis Hail Damage Experts

Indiana's severe weather can cause significant damage that isn't always visible from the ground. Hail as small as a quarter can compromise your roof's integrity, leading to leaks and costly interior damage if left unaddressed.

Our certified hail damage specialists know exactly what to look for. We document every impact, crack, and damaged shingle to ensure your insurance claim covers all necessary repairs.

Signs of Hail Damage:

  • Dents or bruises on shingles
  • Missing or cracked shingles
  • Granule loss (check gutters for accumulation)
  • Dented gutters, vents, or flashing
  • Damage to outdoor AC units or vehicles

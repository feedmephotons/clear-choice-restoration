Our Story

Family-Owned & Operated Since 2001

What started as a small family business over two decades ago has grown into one of the Midwest's most trusted names in roofing and restoration. But even as we've grown, one thing has never changed: our commitment to treating every customer like family.

Clear Choice Restoration was founded on a simple principle: provide honest, quality work at fair prices, and always put the customer first. We understand that your home is your biggest investment, and we treat it with the care and respect it deserves.

When we founded our company in 2001, we made a commitment to excellence that continues to drive everything we do today. From our skilled craftsmen to our dedicated office staff, every member of our team shares the same goal: your complete satisfaction.

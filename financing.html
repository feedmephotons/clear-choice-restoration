Common Questions

Financing FAQ

What credit score do I need?

We work with multiple lenders to accommodate various credit situations. While better credit typically means better rates, we have options for most credit profiles. The best way to find out is to apply—it only takes a few minutes and won't affect your credit score.

Is there a minimum or maximum loan amount?

Financing is available for projects ranging from $1,000 to $100,000, covering everything from minor repairs to complete roof replacements and multi-project renovations.

How long does approval take?

Most applications receive a decision within minutes. Once approved, you can proceed with your project right away.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Absolutely. There are no prepayment penalties. You can pay off your balance at any time without any additional fees.

What if I have insurance coverage?

If insurance covers part of your project, you can finance just the remaining balance—your deductible and any upgrades you choose.

Do you offer 0% financing?

We occasionally offer promotional 0% APR financing for qualified buyers. Contact us to learn about current promotions and special offers.

Smart Financing

Combining Insurance & Financing

If you have storm damage, your homeowner's insurance may cover most of your roof replacement. In many cases, you'll only need to pay your deductible out of pocket.

For homeowners who want to:

  • Finance their insurance deductible
  • Upgrade to premium shingles beyond what insurance covers
  • Add gutters, siding, or other improvements
  • Complete additional home exterior projects

Our financing options make it easy to get everything done at once with one affordable monthly payment.

Already Have Insurance?
